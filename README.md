# Evaluating the Impact of AI-Driven Large Language Models on Teaching Practices in Further Education (UK)

## Project Overview

This project investigates the impact of Large Language Models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT, on teaching practices in UK Further Education institutions.

The study combines quantitative and qualitative research methods to analyse educators' perceptions, adoption patterns, benefits, challenges, and future expectations regarding AI-assisted teaching.

## Objectives

* Examine the adoption of AI tools among Further Education teachers.
* Analyse perceived benefits and challenges of LLMs in education.
* Explore relationships between teaching experience and AI usage.
* Provide evidence-based recommendations for educational institutions.

## Dataset

Data was collected through an online survey distributed to Further Education educators in the United Kingdom.

### Variables Analysed

* Teaching experience
* Frequency of AI usage
* Perceived usefulness of AI tools
* Concerns regarding AI implementation
* Future adoption intentions

## Key Findings

* Most educators reported positive experiences with AI-assisted teaching.
* AI tools improved lesson preparation efficiency.
* Concerns remained regarding academic integrity and overreliance on AI.
* Digital literacy influenced adoption rates.
